One World. One Winner.

Tournament Progression

  1. Monday

    Everyone in the world (including web2 users) participates in races. Survivors receive NFTs.

  2. Tuesday

    Requires Monday NFT to participate. Winners receive Tuesday NFTs.

  3. Wednesday

    Requires Tuesday NFT to participate. Winners receive Wednesday NFTs.

  4. Thursday

    Requires Wednesday NFT to participate. Winners receive Thursday NFTs.

  5. Friday

    Requires Thursday NFT to participate. Winners receive Friday NFTs.

  6. Saturday

    Requires Friday NFT to participate. Winners receive Saturday NFTs.

  7. Sunday

    Final races. One global winner receives exclusive privileges.

Key Features

  1. Global Participation

    Every game is important because every single player on Earth is fighting each other in a single worldwide elimination tournament.

  2. NFT Pass Market

    Players can either play to win and sell NFTs or buy them to stay in the race.

  3. Winner Privileges

    Sunday winner receives real monetary value along with a personal message for the next week’s race that he can sell as a slot to advertisers.

Games are short (one minute) and simple

No NPCs, no missions — the only task is to survive and not get eaten.
